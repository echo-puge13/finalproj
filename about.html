RG STREETWEAR GARAGE

RG Streetwear Garage officially opened its doors on January 21, 2023, marking the realization of our dream to bring quality streetwear products to our local community. Located at Brgy. 16 Quiling Norte, Batac City, Ilocos Norte, along MacArthur Highway, our shop is conveniently situated in front of Saviors Christian School, beside RJVL Store and Helen's Eatery. Open daily from 10 AM to 8 PM, we invite you to visit and explore our collection. Thank you for supporting our journey and being part of our growing community! Since our opening, RG Streetwear Garage has become a hub for stylish and affordable streetwear that resonates with the local culture and trends. We are committed to providing exceptional customer service and continuously updating our collection to meet the evolving tastes of our community.
